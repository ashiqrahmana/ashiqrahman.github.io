BakerBot - Smart Kitchen Robot | 202141060759 · Issued Dec 25, 2022

This innovative machine has been designed to be fully automated and compact, with the ability to make, stack, and store stuffed parathas/chapatis using only wheat and water as ingredients. With its compact size and low cost, the baker bot is set to revolutionize the way food is prepared and served, offering a convenient and affordable solution for households and businesses alike.

The key features of the baker bot are:

  • Fully automated
  • Compact size
  • Low-cost
  • Intelligent IoT control
  • Uses only wheat and water as ingredients
  • Makes, stacks, and stores finished products in hotboxes
